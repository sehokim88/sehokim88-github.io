Usefulness Understanding

Gaining accurate insight into your predicted sleep quality, specifically total number of deep sleep hours, prior to the bed time each day based on the prediction model will grant users an ability to plan sleep schedule and adjust daytime behaviors in order to have better control over quality of the following day.

How The Product Works

Input: desired deep sleep amount for tonight

Input: desired awake time

Input: disturbance parameters

Input: sleep & activity tracker data upto 2 hours prior to expected asleep


Output: asleep time necessary in order to achieve desired hours of deep sleep
